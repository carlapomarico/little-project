Ramen Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200 gr minced pork meat
  • 5 cups chicken stock
  • 4 stalks green onion
  • 1/2 cloves garlic
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 400 gr ramen noodles
  • Sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Chop the garlic.
  2. Place a pan on medium heat and add oil, garlic and green onion.
  3. When caramelized, add everything except for noodles and bring to boil.
  4. Add noodles, cook for another minutes, then serve.
